HOME

On the Home screen, the connection is made automatically , however, if a problem occurs, and ROOM KITCHEN buttons are unavailable. The LOGON button tells you that you can restart the connection. HELP takes you to the documentation.

KITCHEN

The kitchen screen , allows creating the database of a new flat and inventory management by adding or removing an available dish.

DINING

The screen room consists of two screens :

  • The list of current orders and create a new order.
  • The following lists all the dishes with information such as the quantity available or the quantity ordered. A button list only the ordered dishes.
